Elemental Skill

Creates Solar Isotoma:

Enemy within the field takes DMG, Deals Geo DMG based on Albedo's DEF

Solar Isotoma

  • Procs every 2s
  • Can be aimed if held
  • If stood on the platform, Lifts up the character slightly
  • Can be destroyed

    CD: 4s

    Elemental Burst

    A4 C2 C5

  • If Solar Isotoma is present, Burst procs 7 times

    • Energy Cost: 40

    Normal Attack

    A1

    Performs 5 attacks

    Elemental Skill

    A4 C1 C2 C3 C6

    Creates 1 Chisel Light-Mirror. Mirrors grants Dendro Infusion and Projection Attacks

    Holding mode teleports to aimed location

    Chisel Light-Mirror:

  • Creates 2 Mirrors if no Mirrors exists on cast
  • Switching characters expires existing Mirrors

    • Mirror Duration: 4s

    Max Mirrors: 3

    Projection Attacks:

    Deals Dendro DMG alongside of Normal Attack. DMG depends on the number of Mirrors present

  • 1 Mirror: Deals 1x Dendro DMG based on ATK and EM
  • 2 Mirrors: Deals 2x Dendro DMG based on ATK and EM
  • 3 Mirrors: Deals 3x Dendro DMG based on ATK and EM

    • Projection Attack Interval: 1.6s

    CD: 18s

    Elemental Burst

    A4 C4 C5 C6

    Consumes current Mirrors and creates an AoE Field that deals Dendro DMG instances

    Burst depends on the number of Mirrors consumed

  • Deals 4/6/8/10 instances every 0/1/2/3 Mirrors consumed
  • Creates 3/2/1/0 Mirrors every 0/1/2/3 Mirrors consumed (Creates Mirrors 2s after Burst)

    • Energy Cost: 70

    CD: 18s

    Ascension Passive 1

    Charged and Plunge Attacks creates 1 Mirror

    CD: 12

    Ascension Passive 4

    Increases Projection Attack and Burst DMG by 0.1% for every 1EM

    Max DMG Bonus: 100%/1000EM

  • Charged Attack consumes stacks to deal consecutive attacks

  • Gaining stacks refreshes duration
  • Reaching max stacks glows the indicator

    • Max stacks: 5

    Stack duration: 60s

  • Gives 1 stack on expire or killed

    • Duration: 6s

    CD: 10s

  • Converts All Normal attacks to Geo DMG

  • Switching character cancels state

    • Energy Cost: X

    CD: X

  • Each hit increases the next attack's ATK SPD by 10% (Max 30%)
