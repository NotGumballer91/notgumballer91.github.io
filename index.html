Elemental Skill

Any enemy within the field takes damage, Deals Geo DMG based on Albedo's DEF (Transient Blossom)

  • Procs every 2 seconds
  • Can be aimed if held
  • If stood on the platform, Lifts up the character a certain height
  • Solar Isotoma can be destroyed

    • Duration: 30s

    CD: 4s

    Elemental Burst

    Deals AoE Geo DMG based on Albedo's ATK

  • If Solartoma is present, It will proc 7 more times

    • Energy Cost: 40

    CD: 12s

    Ascension Passive 1

    Solar Isotoma deals 25% more damage to enemies below 50% HP

    Ascension Passive 4

    Using Burst gives all party members 125 EM for 10s

    Normal Attack

    Performs 5 rapid attacks

    Elemental Skill

    Creates 1 Chisel Light-Mirror, Grants Dendro Infusion and Projection Attacks

    Chisel Light-Mirror:

    Grants Dendro Infusion

  • If there are no Mirrors on cast, Creates 2 Mirrors
  • Can aim and teleport by Holding skill

    • Mirror Duration: 4s

    Projection Attacks:

    Deals Dendro DMG alongside of Normal Attack. DMG depends on the number of Mirrors present

  • 1 Mirror: Deals 1x Dendro DMG based on ATK and EM
  • 2 Mirrors: Deals 2x Dendro DMG based on ATK and EM
  • 3 Mirrors: Deals 3x Dendro DMG based on ATK and EM

    • Projection Attack Interval: 1.6s

    CD: 18s

    Elemental Burst

    Consumes current Mirrors and creates Dendro Damaging AoE Field. Duration depends on the number of Mirrors consumed

  • Instances are 4/6/8/10 if 0/1/2/3 Mirrors consumed

    • 2 seconds after Burst:

  • Creates 3/2/1/0 Mirrors if 0/1/2/3 Mirror were consumed

    • Energy Cost: 70

    CD: 18s

    Ascension Passive 1

    Charged and Plunging Attack creates 1 Mirror

    CD: 12

    Ascension Passive 4

    Increases Projection Attack and Burst DMG by 0.1% for every 1EM

    Max DMG Bonus: 100%/1000EM

    Normal Attack

    Performs 5 rapid attacks

    Charged attacks does multiple slashes

  • Deals 3 instances

    • Charged attack has medium range

  • Hits all enemies if within range

    • Elemental Skill

    Deals Cryo DMG in AoE

  • Launches enemies upwards
  • Cannot launch heavy enemies

    • CD: 10s

    Elemental Burst

    Summons Snowstorm Blade, cutting enemies and dealing AoE Cryo DMG over time

  • Has 19 cutting instances
  • Can push enemies away

    • Energy Cost: 80

    CD: 20s

    Alternative Sprint

    Toggles between Frozen Fog Cloak:

  • Activation Stamina costs 10
  • Drains 10 stamina movement per second

    • Grants Cryo infusion

    Infusion Duration: 5s

    Ascension Passive 1

    After using Skill, Normal and Charged attacks deals 30% more damage

    Duration: 6s

    Ascension Passive 4

    Hitting enemies with Alt sprint:

  • Restores 10 stamina
  • +18% Cryo DMG Bonus

    • Duration: 10s

    Normal Attack

    Performs 4 Dendro attacks

    Normal attacks has melee range

    Elemental Skill

    Sends a Dendro attack, hitting 3 enemies

    Heals entire party based on Baizhu's Max HP upon return

    Duration: 30s

    CD: 10s

    Elemental Burst

    Generates a Dendro Shield that heals and attacks when it expires/shattered

    Shield Duration: 2.5s

    Duration: 14s

    Energy Cost: 80

    CD: 20s

    Ascension Passive 1

    Baizhu gains the following effects:

  • Active character has less than 50% HP: 20% Healing Bonus
  • Active character has more than 50% HP: 25% Dendro DMG Bonus

    • Ascension Passive 4

    Baizhu's Shield increases active character's Dendro-reaction DMG for every Baizhu's 1000HP:

  • 2% for Burning, Bloom, Hyperbloom and Burgeon (Max 100%)
  • 0.8% for Aggravate and Spread (Max 40%)

    • Normal Attack

    Performs 4 strikes

    Elemental Skill

    Deals AoE Pyro DMG and Creates Fiery Sanctum Field:

    Fiery Sanctum:

    Can be retrieved and deployed

  • Field deals Pyro DMG when enemies taken DMG within the field
  • Increases Character's Interrupt Resistance
  • All Damage mitigated to Dehya

    • Max Mitigation: 200% Dehya's Max HP

    Duration: 12s

    CD: 20s

    Elemental Burst

    Enters Blazing Lioness state, Dealing consecutive Pyro DMG based on Dehya's ATK + Max HP

    Blazing Lioness:

  • Overrides Normal Attack and Skill
  • Retrieves Fiery Sanctum and redeploys the field at the end of it
  • Jumping cancels Blazing Lioness state
  • Sprint-Jumping redeploys Fiery Sanctum immediately

    • Duration: 4s

    Energy Cost: 70

    CD: 18s

    Ascension Passive 1

    Retrieving Fiery Sanctum through Skill/Burst:

  • Dehya takes 60% less DMG from mitigation
  • Duration: 6s

  • CD: 2s

    Deploying Fiery Sanctum:

  • Increases Interrupt Resistance further
  • CD: 18s

    • Ascension Passive 4

    If Dehya's HP is less than 40%:

  • Recovers 20% of her Max HP
  • Recovers 6% of her Max HP for 10s

    • CD: 20s

    Normal Attack

    Performs 4 attacks

    Charged Attack toggles between Pneuma and Ousia Arkhe states

    Normal Attack procs Arkhe attack depending on the state

    Arkhe Attack CD: 6s

  • Each state has indepedent CDs

    • Elemental Skill

    Creates Salon Members depending on current Arkhe Alignment

  • Can toggle between Arkhe states

    • Ousia:

    Creates 3 Sea Creatures, Attacks nearby enemies, Dealing Hydro DMG based on Furina's Max HP
  • Drains entire party's HP until 50%
  • Sea Creatures DMG increases by 110%/120%/130%/140% for every 1/2/3/4 party member HP drained
  • Sea Creatures follows your character

    • Pneuma:

    Creates a Water Singer that heals active character based on Furina's Max HP
  • Water Singer is stationary

    • Duration: 30s

    CD: 20s

    Elemental Burst

    Grants the entire team Fanfare Points based on HP% change throughout the duration

    Fanfare points:

  • DMG Increase for every HP% change
  • Healing Bonus for every HP% change

    • Max Fanfare: 300

    Duration: 18s

    Energy Cost: 60

    CD: 15s

    Ascension Passive 1

    Any healing besides Furina overflows, Heals entire party 2% of their Max HP

    Healing Interval: 2s

    Healing Duration: 4s

    Ascension Passive 4

    Every 1000HP Furina has:

  • Sea Creatures deals 0.7% more DMG (Max 28%/40000HP)
  • Water Singer heals 0.4% faster (Max 16%/40000HP)

    All attacks are considered as Heavy attacks

    Elemental Skill

  • Mines can be pulled

    • Has 2 charges

    CD: 20s

  • Does not work off-field

    • Duration: 10s

    Energy Cost: 60

    CD: 15

  • Charged Attack deals 50% more DMG
  • Charged Attack does not consume stamina

  • Works off-field

  • Deals another tick of Max HP to enemies hit by the Burst

  • Converts Bloom reaction to Bountiful Cores
  • Increases all party member's EM by 100 when taking Dendro DMG

    • Bountiful Cores:

  • Deals Large AoE Dendro DMG
  • Explodes immediately
  • Cannot react with other elements

    • Ascension Passive 4

    Increases Bountiful Cores DMG by 9% for every 1000HP above 30000HP Nilou's Max HP

    Max DMG Bonus: 400% (74444HP)

    Performs 5 attacks

    Elemental Skill

    Deals AoE Electro DMG and Summons an Eye of Stormy Judgment for all party members

  • Deals coordinated Electro DMG attacks on every hit
  • Increases Elemental Burst DMG based on Energy Cost

    • Coordinated Attack Interval: 0.9s

  • Co-op party member's Coordinated Attacks only deals 20% DMG

    • Duration: 25s

    CD: 10s

    Consumes all Resolve stacks and enters Musou Isshin state, Dealing Huge Electro DMG

  • All Bursts except Raiden Shogun, gives Resolve Stacks based on their Energy consumed
  • Resolve Stacks increases Musou Isshin and Burst DMG

  • Overrides All Normal Attacks with Electro DMG
  • Increased Interrupt Resistance and Immune to Electro-Charged reaction DMG
  • Restores entire party's energy every hit
  • Energy Restortion only restores 5 times and has 1s CD

    • Musou Isshin Duration: 7s

    Max Resolve Stacks: 60

    Energy Cost: 90

    CD: 18s

    CD: 3s

  • Increases Energy Restoration during Musou Isshin by 0.6%
  • Increases Electro DMG Bonus by 0.4%

    Charged shot applies Riptide

    Riptide effects acts different depending on states

    Riptide:

  • Riptide Flash: Enemies hit with Charged Shot
  • Riptide Burst: Enemies killed with Riptide applies Riptide to other nearby enemies

    • Riptide Duration: 10s


    Elemental Skill

    Switches into Melee stance:

  • Performs 6 attacks
  • Normal attacks deals Hydro DMG
  • Enemies hit with Riptide takes additional Riptide Slash DMG
  • CD depends how long you've been in the Melee stance

    • Duration: 30s

    CD: 6-36s

    Max CD: 45s

    Elemental Burst

    Deals Hydro AoE DMG depending on stance

    Melee stance:

  • Performs huge AoE Hydro DMG around Tartaglia
  • Enemies hit with Riptide takes additional Riptide Blast DMG

    • Ranged stance:

  • Deals Hydro AoE DMG in front of Tartaglia
  • Applies Riptide status
  • Returns 20 Energy

    • Energy Cost: 60

    CD: 15s

    Increases Riptide duration by 8s

    During Melee stance, the following applies Riptide to enemies:

  • Crit Hit
  • Normal Attack
  • Charged Attack

    Performs 3 Anemo attacks

    Charged Attack has medium range

    Enters Windfavored state. Uses separate stamina

    Running out of stamina ends state

    Windfavored:

  • Grants flight
  • Normal/Charged Attacks deals additional Anemo DMG
  • Charged Attack does not consume stamina
  • Drains stamina passively
  • Sprinting and ascending consumes more stamina

    • Windfavored Stamina: 100

    CD: 6s

    Elemental Burst

    Deals AoE Anemo DMG in front of Wanderer

  • Has 5 instances
  • Casting Burst during Windfavored ends the state

    • Energy Cost: 60

    CD: 15s

    Ascension Passive 1

    Swirling elements on skill cast grants bonus effects:

  • Hydro: Increases Windfavored Stamina by 20
  • Pyro: Increases ATK by 30%
  • Cryo: Increases Crit Rate by 20%
  • Electro: Normal/Charged Attacks restores 0.8 energy (CD: 0.2s)

    • Max Effects: 2

    Ascension Passive 4

    During Windfavored state, Normal/Charged Attacks has 16% chance to a gain a charge:

  • Fires 4 wind arrows, dealing 35% Anemo DMG 35% of ATK each
  • Increases chance by 12% when charge is not gained
  • Charge does not consume Windfavored stamina

    • Use sprint to use the charge during Windfavored state
